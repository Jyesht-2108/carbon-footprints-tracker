## ğŸ¯ Design Principles

### Dark Mode Philosophy
1. **Reduce Eye Strain**: Low brightness for night use
2. **Professional Look**: Sophisticated navy palette
3. **High Contrast**: White text on dark backgrounds
4. **Ambient Lighting**: Subtle glowing effects
5. **Glass Morphism**: Translucent layered cards

### Light Mode Philosophy
1. **Comfortable Reading**: High brightness for day use
2. **Clean & Fresh**: Airy white palette
3. **Clear Hierarchy**: Dark text on light backgrounds
4. **Subtle Depth**: Soft shadows
5. **Vibrant Accents**: Colorful highlights
